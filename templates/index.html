How It Works
A three-stage pipeline from screening to intervention planning.
01
🔬
Clinically Accurate Scoring
Q-CHAT-10 items A1–A9 score risk when a positive behaviour is absent. Item A10 is reverse-scored: it scores risk when atypical blank staring is present. AQ-10 for older children uses agree/disagree direction per item. The model is trained and infers with these correct polarities.
02
⚙️
Ensemble Prediction
XGBoost + Random Forest + Gradient Boosting + Logistic Regression soft-vote ensemble with SMOTE oversampling. Trained on 3,759 samples across three age cohorts (toddler, early child, adolescent).
03
🎯
Intervention Planning
Based on risk level, age group, and key factors, the system suggests personalized interventions — from early behavioral therapy for toddlers to CBT for adolescents.
